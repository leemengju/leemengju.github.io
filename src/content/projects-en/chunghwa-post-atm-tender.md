## Background

Aligned with Chunghwa Post's digital development roadmap, this tender aimed to extend a century of postal trust and build a diverse, inclusive, and sustainable postal ecosystem, redesigning the new ATM visual style and interaction experience from the user's perspective. The company was unfamiliar with financial-ATM business, the tender timeline was tight, and both project leads were occupied with other projects, making real-time communication and coordination difficult.

## Scope

Over three months, delivered the complete Chunghwa Post tender proposal, an interface-design optimization plan for core ATM functions, and the preparation for the oral defense and unified Q&A in the review stage.

## Challenges

Unfamiliar with financial-ATM business and under a compressed schedule, the team had to simultaneously produce high-quality proposal content and an oral-defense response strategy, while coordinating the company's limited internal staffing.

## Contributions

- Compiled and wrote the company materials and the researchers' organizational structure.
- Wrote the ATM design plan, ensuring the soundness of the plan and the feasibility of its implementation.
- Owned Q&A planning and rehearsals — drew up a detailed oral-defense plan and ran multiple rehearsals to ensure a polished presentation and accurate unified Q&A answers.

## Impact

The team completed the tender and placed 2nd; through the retrospective, we consolidated and optimized the company's proposal deck template and distilled tender guidelines and a standard process as reference for similar future cases.
